Who I Am

My passion for technology began at the age of 10; being young, with English knowledge in a hispanic household meant I had to assist my parents with any tasks they could not understand due to the language barrier.

Eventually this meant fixing router, and computer issues. With the help of Youtube and Google I was always able to find a solution. Thus becoming the "mega brain", as they called me, in my home.

As an adult now, I have become to love the feeling of finding a solution to a technical problem. The joy I feel after spending hours on a specific problem and finally finding a solution, it is unreal. As I worked through courses on my own time, during college, and through bootcamp with Multiverse, I knew this is what I wanted to do as a career. As a software engineer I know I will always be learning, improving my work, and, more importantly, happy especially when solving a problems for my team.
